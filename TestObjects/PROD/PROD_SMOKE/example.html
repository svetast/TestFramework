RENTAL AGREEMENT

1. Rental Terms and Right to Acquire Ownership.

You hereby agree to rent the Rental Property (set of 4 new automobile tires described on Disclosure Page) from us, and we hereby agree to rent the Rental Property to you, for the Initial Rental Term ({{LeasePeriodDays}} days) commencing on the date of execution of this Rental Agreement.  After you have completed the Initial Rental Period, this Rental Agreement shall continue on a Rental Period-to-Rental Period basis thereafter for up to a total of {{RemainingPayments}} additional Rental Periods unless you terminate this Rental Agreement.  If you have made all of the Periodic Rental Payments due us for a total of {{LeaseTerm}} consecutive Rental Periods in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Rental Agreement, we will convey ownership of the Rental Property to at the end of the {{LeaseTerm}} Rental Periods at no additional cost.

2. Voluntary Termination of Rental Agreement.

You may terminate this Rental Agreement at any time without penalty or further obligation prior to the beginning of the next successive Rental Period that has not been renewed and paid for by returning the Rental Property (set of 4 tires) to us in good condition (subject to normal wear and tear) at the installation location specified in this Agreement. If we are required to remove and dismount the tires, you agree to pay a service fee of $9.00 per tire ($36.00 per set of 4 tires), and you will be responsible for providing your own replacement tires and paying a fee of $9.00 per tire ($36.00 per set) to have them remounted on your rims and installed on your vehicle. 

3.  Ownership/Risk of Loss.

You do not own the property you are renting. We own the Rental Property being rented to you.  You are responsible and liable for paying us the Fair Market Value if the Rental Property is lost, stolen or destroyed, regardless of fault. You are also responsible for any repairs that aren’t covered by any applicable manufacturer’s warranty or the Road Hazard Benefit we have provided you under Paragraph 5 in this Agreement. While the Rental Property is in your possession, you may not sell, assign, mortgage, pawn, pledge, encumber, or otherwise dispose of the Rental Property. You also may not remove the Rental Property from the vehicle on which the Rental Property was installed (identified by VIN number on Disclosure Page) without our written consent.

4. Manufacturer’s Warranty.

The Rental Property you are renting (set of 4 new automobile tires described on Disclosure Page) will be mounted on the rims of the vehicle identified by VIN number on the Disclosure Page, balanced and installed on the vehicle by our service provider at the location specified in this Agreement, and will be subject to the specific manufacturer’s warranties that cover the Rental Property (tires). If there is damage to the Rental Property which is covered by a manufacturer’s warranty, we will have the damage repaired under the terms and conditions of the warranty by our service provider at the location specified in this Agreement. If you acquire ownership by satisfactorily completing the number of Periodic Rental Payments specified in this Rental Agreement, the manufacturer’s warranty will be transferred to you.

5. Road Hazard Benefit.

We are providing to you at no additional cost during the term of this Rental Agreement a Road Hazard Benefit which will entitle you to have the Rental Property (set of 4 new automobile tires described on Disclosure Page) rotated at a minimum of 5,000 mile intervals free of charge by our service provider at the location specified in this Agreement for the term of this Rental Agreement. Further, we will pay for the repair of any tire caused to go flat (and which is fully repairable) as a result of a puncture by our service provider at the location specified in this Agreement at no charge. We will replace any tire that is damaged beyond repair, and which is not otherwise covered by a manufacturer’s warranty, by our service provider at the location specified in this Agreement with an identical new tire for a fixed cost of $59.00 (including mounting, balancing and installation) to you such that any replacement tire(s) will become part of the Rental Property for which you are entitled to acquire ownership at the successful conclusion of the Rental Agreement, or to return to us if you voluntarily terminate the Rental Agreement.

6.  Other Charges for Late Payments and Collection Actions.

If you fail to pay the Rental Payment due at the beginning of each renewed Rental Period, we may assess a late payment fee of $25 for any payment not received within five (5) business days from the date of the beginning of the Rental Period. A fee of $25.00 will be assessed for any dishonored check, money order or ACH draft. If you do not return the Rental Property following the conclusion of the Initial Rental Period, or any subsequent Rental Period whereby you fail or decline to renew and pay for the next consecutive Rental Period, you agree to permit us to take any and all legal actions necessary to collect our unpaid Rental Payments and/or recover our Rental Property including attorney’s fees permitted by law.

7.  Reinstatement of Terminated Rental Agreement.

If you have declined or failed to renew and pay for any subsequent Rental Period during the Rental Term, and have returned the Rental Property to us or caused us to recover the Rental Property, you have the right to reinstate this Rental Agreement as follows:

(a) if you have paid seventeen or fewer Periodic Rental Payments at the time of the voluntary or involuntary termination of this Rental Agreement, you have the right to reinstate this Rental Agreement during a period of no more than two Periodic Rental Periods (4 weeks) following the conclusion of the prior Rental Term by paying to us all missed consecutive Periodic Rental Payments plus a $10.00 reinstatement charge for each missed Periodic Rental Payment, and a fee of $72.00 to have the original Rental Property reinstalled on the original vehicle identified by VIN number on Disclosure Page.

(b) if you have paid eighteen or more Periodic Rental Payments at the time of the voluntary or involuntary termination of this Rental Agreement, you have the right to reinstate this Rental Agreement during a period of no more than four Periodic Rental Periods (8 weeks) following the conclusion of the prior Rental Term by paying to us all missed consecutive Periodic Rental Payments plus a $10.00 reinstatement charge for each missed Periodic Rental Payment, and a fee of $72.00 to have the original Rental Property reinstalled on the original vehicle identified by VIN number on Disclosure Page. Reinstatement as provided above will continue this Rental Agreement in accordance with its original terms, and preserve your right to acquire ownership of the Rental Property. You agree that we will not be required to retain the original Rental Property returned to or recovered by us for a period in excess of two Rental Periods (4 weeks) in order to provide for a Reinstatement of a Terminated Rental Agreement under subparagraph (a) above; or for a period in excess of four Rental Periods (8 weeks) in order to provide for a Reinstatement of a Terminated Rental Agreement under subparagraph (b) above.

You have the right to reinstatement under the terms of this paragraph even if we have had to take action to recover the Rental Property.

8. Authorization to Request Information.

You hereby authorize us to request information from your employer, landlord, bank and any other party listed on your application to us for this Rental Agreement, and you hereby authorize all such parties to release such requested information to us. We agree not to contact any credit reporting agency regarding your credit history, FICO scores, etc., or to report any information to any credit bureau relating to this Rental Agreement.

9.  Obligation to Pay and Payment Method.

You shall pay to us all amounts due under the terms of this Rental Agreement commencing with the Initial Rental Payment specified on the Disclosure Page, and all Rental Payments due for successive Rental Periods.  At your option, we will adjust the beginning date of all subsequent Rental Periods to coincide with the dates on which you are regularly paid by your employer (or receive other monthly income) each month for your convenience.

You agree to authorize us to draft (“ACH”) your checking or debt card account specified under your signature on this Rental Agreement for the Initial Rental Period as of the date of your execution of this Rental Agreement, and for all successive Periodic Rental Periods on the dates due for all successive Rental Periods to a maximum of {{LeaseTerm}} total Rental Periods unless you terminate this Rental Agreement prior to the completion of a total of {{LeaseTerm}} Rental Periods. You further agree to notify us if you change your bank or debit card account during the term of this Rental Agreement, and agree to the continuing collection of your rental payments by ACH draft of any successor accounts.

You understand that we do not accept payments made by credit card or in cash in order to provide the lowest cost rent-to-own services to you. In the event that an exception is made to collect a payment from you by credit card, or by money order, you agree to pay an additional service charge of $2.50 per such transaction.

10.  Governing Law, Arbitration and Amendments. 

You and we agree that this Rental Agreement shall be construed and enforced under the laws of the State of Nevada, excluding Nevada’s choice-of-law principles, and all claims relating to or arising out of this Rental Agreement, or the breach thereof, whether sounding in contract, tort or otherwise, shall likewise be governed by the laws of the State of Nevada, excluding Nevada’s choice-of-law principles.  If you or we assert a controversy or claim arising out of or relating to this Rental Agreement, or the breach thereof, you and we agree that such matters shall be settled exclusively by arbitration in accordance with the Commercial Arbitration Rules of the American Arbitration Association (“AAA”), Washington, DC, and judgment upon the award rendered by the arbitrator may be entered in any court located in any jurisdiction.  You and we expressly consent to the binding arbitral jurisdiction of the AAA in any and all proceedings and the respective entry of award thereof as judgment in any of the permitted courts.  Such arbitration shall be conducted by a single arbitrator appointed by the AAA who shall be an attorney with not less than ten years of corporate law experience.  You and we hereby irrevocably and unconditionally waive any objection to the laying of venue of any such proceeding and hereby further irrevocably and unconditionally waive and agree not to plead or claim in any such court that any such proceeding brought in any such court has been brought in an inconvenient forum. You and we agree that no amendment, modification, termination or waiver of any provision of this Rental Agreement, nor consent to any departure by you or us from this Rental Agreement, shall in any event be effective unless the same shall be in writing and signed by each of us.

Nothing in this Paragraph 9 shall affect our right to recover the Rental Property which we own if you have not made your Periodic Rental Payment(s) by any and all lawful means as agreed to in Paragraph 6 above; or to proceed with non-judicial collection actions for any and all payments due us.

11. Miscellaneous Matters

Alignment of your vehicle’s wheels is not provided for under this Rental Agreement. As an additional benefit for you, we will have an alignment service provided to you by our service provider at the location specified in this Agreement for a fixed cost of $50.00 (plus any applicable taxes) to be performed at the time of the installation of the Rental Property. If you elect to utilize this benefit, this cost must be paid separately against an invoice which is not a part of this Rental Contract.
